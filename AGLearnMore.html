About the Books

We offer 3 books useful for not only for working accountants and auditors, but also for accounting students and those studying for the CPA exam:


Wiley GAAP 2019: Interpretation and Application of Generally Accepted Accounting (This title includes a valuable online financial statement disclosure checklist. The title is also available with a CD-ROM version or as a Kindle ebook.)


Wiley GAAS 2019: Covering all SASs, SSAEs, SSARSs and interpretations.


Revenue Recognition plus Website: Understanding the New Standard (This title includes access to a dedicated website with online implementation tools and technical updates. The title is also available as a Kindle ebook.)
